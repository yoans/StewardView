Security You Can
Present to Your Elders

Church finances require the highest level of trust. StewardView is built for that.

🔐

Complete Data Isolation

Your church's data is logically isolated from every other congregation. No cross-tenant access is possible.

🔑

JWT Authentication + Role-Based Access

Industry-standard tokens. Every user has a role — Admin, Treasurer, Elder, Finance Committee, or Viewer. Access is enforced server-side.

📜

Immutable Audit Log

Every change is logged with who made it, when, and what changed. Nothing can be quietly undone.

🏦

Bank-Grade Plaid Integration

Banking credentials are never stored — Plaid handles all bank authentication with 256-bit encryption.

Dual-Admin Requirement

The system enforces a minimum of two active admin accounts — protecting against single-point-of-failure access control.
